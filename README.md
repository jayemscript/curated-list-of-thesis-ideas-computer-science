# Curated List of Computer Science Thesis Ideas

A curated collection of **AI-generated Computer Science thesis ideas** gathered from multiple Large Language Models (LLMs). This repository aims to help students, researchers, and educators discover potential undergraduate thesis topics across various Computer Science disciplines.

> [!WARNING]
> **These ideas are AI-generated and may contain inaccurate, impractical, duplicated, or non-novel research topics.**
>
> **Do not use any idea directly as your thesis proposal.** Always conduct a thorough literature review, verify that the problem has not already been extensively solved, evaluate its feasibility, and consult your thesis adviser before proceeding.

## Available Collections

* **[CHATGPT.md](CHATGPT.md)** — Thesis ideas generated using ChatGPT.
* **[QWEN.md](QWEN.md)** — Thesis ideas generated using Qwen.

## Purpose

This repository is intended to:

* Provide inspiration for undergraduate Computer Science thesis topics.
* Explore emerging technologies and interdisciplinary research areas.
* Help students brainstorm research directions before conducting formal literature reviews.

## Disclaimer

The maintainers do **not** guarantee the originality, correctness, feasibility, or academic acceptance of any thesis idea in this repository. Every idea should be independently validated through reputable academic sources such as IEEE Xplore, ACM Digital Library, Springer, ScienceDirect, Google Scholar, and consultation with your research adviser.

Contributions, improvements, and corrections are always welcome.
